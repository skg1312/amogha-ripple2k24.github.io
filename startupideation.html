Startup Ideation

Instructions

    Round 1 - PPT Submission:
    Submit concise PowerPoint outlining entrepreneurial idea.
    Include problem, solution, market analysis, and impact.
    Feedback provided for refinement.
    Round 2 - Idea Pitching (Elimination Round):
    Live presentation covering key aspects of idea.
    Judges evaluate clarity, persuasiveness, and handling of questions.
    Participants may be eliminated based on performance.
    Round 3 - Idea Validation and Guidance:
    Validate ideas through market research and feasibility studies.
    Receive guidance from top MNCs for refinement.
