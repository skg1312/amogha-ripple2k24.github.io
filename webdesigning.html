Instructions

    This is Team competition consist of two or at most three
    Students should have knowledge on HTML, CSS and Javascript
    It's a three Level contest as follows
    Round 1: Skeleton Design
    Round 2: Styling
    Round 3: JavaScript
    Plagiarism will lead to disqualification.
    Ensure submissions are made on time for each round.
    Participants may receive feedback to aid improvement.
    Judging will be impartial based on set criteria.
    Judges' decisions will be final.
